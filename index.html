How to get to the Reston Office.

There are several options to arrive to the Reston office without driving. The most convinient method is using a combination of the Silver Line Metro and taking either a bus/shuttle or biking the remaining 1 mile

Project Shuttle

In order to commute from the Silver Line Metro to the Reston office, there is a Project Shuttle that can be taken for 8am-5pm work. The Shuttle will also stop at the farther parking lot, in roughly a 20 minute loop. Between 8-4:30 the shuttle will continue to go only between the parking lot and the Reston office.

The shuttle is a white bus that has Reston Limosine written on the side and picks up infront of the Office. There is an additional route that drops off at the Reston Plaza.

Biking to the office

Several routes to bike to the office. From the Wiehle-Reston East Station you can bike taking Sunrise Valley to the Office. The Station has a Secure Bike Room which can be accessed only by RFID tag, and costs an annual membership fee. At the reston office there are Bike Racks availiable inside the parkinglot on the south-east corners on every level.

Fairfax Connector

The options for transit outside of the 8am-5pm business hours, no longer include the project shuttle. For these times, it is possible to take one of the Fairfax Connectors to Reston Parkway a block from the Office. These options decrease as the day goes on, limiting options. There are NO Fairfax Connector busses serving the 10pm shift end on the weekends.
